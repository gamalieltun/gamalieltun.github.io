Who I Am

About Me

Engineer. Educator. Builder. Storyteller.

Gamaliel Hla Tun Gamay

I'm a Computer Engineering graduate from Singapore Polytechnic with a deep obsession for building systems that make a real difference — whether in semiconductor labs, church development offices, or someone's living room.

Currently I serve as Provincial Digital Development Officer for the Church of the Province of Myanmar's Programme Development Department, driving digitalization across humanitarian and development programmes — while also working as an independent technology consultant on the same mission.

Before that I interned at Infineon Technologies Singapore as a Reliability Product Testing Engineer, supporting quality assurance on automotive microcontrollers. I've also taught mathematics, co-founded Coding Geeks Myanmar, and built everything from fuel cost calculators in Burmese to full dashboard platforms running on Cloudflare Workers.
