I build systems that replace chaos with clarity — from church finance dashboards to staff management platforms and IoT prototypes.

Originally from Mandalay, Myanmar, I've been based in Singapore since 2023 studying Computer Engineering at Singapore Polytechnic. In early 2026 I interned at Infineon Technologies on the Reliability Product Testing team — working on automotive-grade microcontrollers in a cleanroom-adjacent SLA environment.

Beyond engineering, I co-founded Coding Geeks Myanmar — a Burmese-language coding education community — and spent years teaching mathematics before pivoting to software. I volunteer at St. Andrew's Cathedral's Myanmar Worship Service, helping with youth events and community organising.

My creative side lives in Gamayio — a studio space I built for film, podcasting, and visual art. I draw obsessively and believe disciplined execution beats raw talent, every time.
